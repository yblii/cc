About us!

Welcome to CropCrafters! CropCrafters focuses on sustainable small-scale farming methods for homes in the Puget Sound region. Methods such as hydroponics and aeroponics can be used in the average household to grow fresh and organic produce. CropCrafters will match you to a growth system based on location and personal requirements. Utilize CropCrafters to switch to sustainable growth and find the perfect system and plants for your home.

How does this work?

Begin by selecting a growth system to implement in your home based on these specifications:
  • Difficulty: How easily the crops grow in the given system
  • Space: How much space in needed to implement the system
  • Maintenance: Time and effort needed to run the system
  • Cost: Relative Cost of the system
